Sun Dried Tomato Mac and Cheese

You'll love this sun dried tomato mac and cheese!

    Ingredients

  • 1 15 oz can coconut milk
  • 3/4 cup sundried tomatoes in oil (it was about 1/4 cup of oil, if that helps)
  • 2 tablespoons miso
  • 1/2 cup nutritional yeast flakes
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 1 lb macaroni
  • Black pepper and something green to garnish, if ya want.

    Steps

  1. Put everything in a blender and, uh, blend. Until totally smooth.
  2. Cook noodles in salted water, drain them and return the pot.
  3. While still hot, add the sauce and mix with a rubber spatula. Taste for salt, I didnâ€™t need any because miso and sundrieds were salty.
  4. Serve!
